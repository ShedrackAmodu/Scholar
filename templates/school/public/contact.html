Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by class level. Generally, we require:
- Completed application form
- Previous academic records
- Birth certificate
- Passport photographs
- Entrance examination (for secondary levels)

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our Admissions Portal. The process is simple:
1. Fill out the online application form
2. Upload required documents
3. Pay application fee
4. Schedule entrance exam (if applicable)

What is the school fee structure?

Our fee structure varies by class level and includes tuition, development levy, and activity fees. Please contact our admissions office or visit the school for detailed fee information. We also offer payment plans and scholarships for qualifying students.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding students and need-based financial aid. Scholarship applications are reviewed twice a year. Contact our financial aid office for more information.
