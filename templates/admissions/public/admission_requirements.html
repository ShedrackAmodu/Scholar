Academic Requirements
  • Minimum literacy level for age
  • English language proficiency
  • Basic numeracy skills
  • Previous academic records
  • Readiness or placement assessment (if required)
Documentation Requirements
  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Transcript/Report Card
  • Medical/Health Certificate
  • Immunization Records
  • Proof of Residence (utility bill/lease)
Health & Medical Requirements
Immunizations Required:
  • DPT (Diphtheria, Pertussis, Tetanus)
  • Polio
  • MMR (Measles, Mumps, Rubella)
  • Hepatitis B
  • Varicella (Chickenpox)
Medical Clearance:
  • Medical exam within the past 12 months
  • Physical fitness assessment (where required)
  • Vision and hearing screening
  • Dental exam recommendation
  • Disclosure of special medical needs
